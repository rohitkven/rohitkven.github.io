Power BI Dashboard

Retail Dynamics Hub:

CATEGORIES, SALES & PROFIT INSIGHTS

Welcome to the Retail Dynamics Hub, an interactive and insightful dashboard that brings the power of data analytics to the heart of retail management. This dynamic tool provides a comprehensive overview of store performance, offering deep insights into product categories, sales trends, and profit margins.

Tabs:

Sales Overview, Product Insights, Stores, Inventory


This dashboard empowers retail professionals, managers, and analysts with the tools needed to make informed decisions. Whether you are optimizing inventory, identifying top-selling products, or strategizing for future success


Slicers:

In addition to Product and Store Slicers, each tab utilizes a "Date" Slicer to drill down between bill cycles. The slicers on every tab are synced together.


DAX Measures:

8 DAX Measures were used across the tabs, here are a few: 

 Avg Order Value = 
DIVIDE([Total Sales], DISTINCTCOUNT('sales'[Sale_ID]), BLANK())
Product Profit Margin = 
DIVIDE(SUM('products'[Product_Price]) 
- 
SUM('products'[Product_Cost]), SUM('products'[Product_Price]), 0)
